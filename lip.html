Computational Methods for Linear Inverse Problems (Spring 2021)

  • Prerequisites:   Numerical Linear Algebra, Probability, Functional Analysis, Statistical Computing, Matlab Programming

  • Classes:   Friday 08:55-11:50am, Lab 108

  • Highlights:   教研结合型课程，探讨反问题计算方法，侧重统计方法、正则化方法
